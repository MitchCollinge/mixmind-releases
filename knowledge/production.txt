# PRODUCTION KNOWLEDGE & SOUND DESIGN

## Sound Design Principles
- Start with a simple patch and layer complexity gradually
- Every element should have a purpose — cut what doesn't serve the track
- Frequency stacking: sub occupies 20–80Hz, bass mid 80–250Hz, upper harmonics 250Hz+
- Movement creates interest: LFO on filter cutoff, pitch automation, volume tremolo

## Genre-Specific Production Notes

### Electronic / Dance / House
- Four-on-the-floor kick: punchy at 60–80Hz, click at 3–5kHz
- Bassline: root note on beat 1, groove on beats 2–4, sidechain to kick
- Chord stabs: short attack, medium decay, filter closed, automate filter open
- Reverb: long pre-delay (30–50ms) to keep elements separated
- Drop: remove everything except kick and bass — then rebuild

### Hip-Hop / Trap
- 808 bass: long decay (2–4s), tuned to key, sidechain lightly to kick
- Snare: heavy reverb, pitched slightly, snap at 5kHz
- Hi-hats: rolls at 1/8, 1/16, 1/32 — velocity variation essential
- Melodic elements: keep sparse, space is the groove
- Sample chops: chop on unexpected beats for interest

### Techno / Industrial
- Kick: distorted, sub-heavy, punchy — layers of sine + noise
- Texture layers: noise, recorded industrial sounds, bitcrushing (Redux)
- Minimal melody — tension and release through filter/volume automation
- Compression: aggressive, pumping side-chain on everything to kick

## Plugin Chains by Element

### Kick (Production)
1. EQ Eight: boost 60Hz, cut 300–500Hz, boost 3–5kHz
2. Compressor: fast attack (0.1ms), medium release (50ms), 4:1

### Bass / 808
1. EQ Eight: high-pass at 30Hz, boost 80–100Hz, cut 200–400Hz
2. Saturator: soft clip mode, subtle drive for harmonics
3. Compressor: slow attack, fast release, 3:1 to tighten

### Pads / Atmospheres
1. Reverb: large hall, pre-delay 40ms, mix 30–50%
2. Auto Filter: LFO on cutoff, slow rate, subtle movement
3. EQ Eight: high-pass at 200Hz, low-pass at 10kHz

## Arrangement Template
- Intro: 8–16 bars, build tension gradually
- Verse/A section: 16–32 bars, establish groove
- Build: 8–16 bars, filter sweeps, rising elements, remove elements
- Drop/Chorus: 16–32 bars, full energy
- Break: 8–16 bars, strip back
- Outro: 8–16 bars, remove elements one by one
